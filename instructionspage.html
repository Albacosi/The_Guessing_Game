The Guessing Game

What is the end goal?

The goal of the game is to attempt to guess the random number in as few attempts as possible !

How the game works:

  1. Start by inputting a positive integer of your choice. This number will be the uppder bound of the randomly generated number
  2. After entering your desired number, a random number will be generated between 1 and the inputted number
  3. Now it is time to guess a number within the specified range
  4. You will be informed if your guess is correct or incorrect
  5. When you finally guess the correct number, your number of guesses will be revealed
  6. The game will track your best score so keep playing and aim for a new high score!
